Nenad Pantic and his work

Nenad Pantic was born in Zagreb, Croatia in 1972. He finished High School of Applied Arts in Zagreb in 1991. and the Academy of Fine Arts in 1997. in Belgrade, Serbia.

He had several attended exibitions of his fantasy based art and one of the most successful was the exibition held at the British Council in Budapest, Hungary in 1999.

Nenad draws his inspiration from fantasy literature combining it with his own imaginary worlds.

Today, Nenad lives with his family in Greystones, Ireland and works as an IT expert.
